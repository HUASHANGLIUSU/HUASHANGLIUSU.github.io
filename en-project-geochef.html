RS-VLM Dataset Intelligence

GeoChef

An integrated discovery and analysis platform for remote-sensing vision-language datasets. Streamlit delivers the visual application, while geochef-skill packages retrieval, comparison, and risk analysis as callable tools.

PythonStreamlitMCP SkillRS-VLMLeakage Detection

Positioning

GeoChef supports RS-VLM dataset research and selection by organizing fragmented information into a searchable, comparable, and traceable knowledge entry point.

Seven Feature Areas

  • Precise filtering and full-text search
  • Side-by-side comparison of up to four datasets
  • Data leakage and provenance analysis
  • Paper assistant, statistics, and NASA imagery

Two-Layer Delivery

  • Streamlit: bilingual visual application and exports
  • geochef-skill: MCP retrieval and analysis tools
  • Shared data source for search, comparison, and risk analysis
  • Reusable in future agent and research workflows

Related Outcome

The platform and its Skill implementation are related outcomes of the university third prize at the 3rd ECNU Digital Literacy and AI Innovation Application Competition.
